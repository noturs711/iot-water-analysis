Water Quality Monitoring System

Welcome to our Water Quality Monitoring System. This project aims to provide real-time data on water quality parameters to ensure safe and clean water for everyone.
Our system continuously monitors Total Dissolved Solids and Turbidity,and provides up-to-date information to help you ensure the water you use is of the highest quality.
